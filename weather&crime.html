01 Project Background

For this Design Sprint, the overarching goal is to create a mobile platform that will help individuals, specifically freelancers & remote workers find public places that they can work from. Some of the main priorities that these individuals are looking for are places where they can take phone calls, quick meetings, and get work done quietly. In terms of the design constraints, the goal of the application is to help users find places that already exist. Additionally, there is a requirement of a monthly fee for users.

02 User's Priorities

  • Access to wifi as most of remote work and freelancers work is done on their computers
  • Prioritize not needing to buy something
  • Public Place with easy access
  • Good Connectivity
  • Limited Crowds
